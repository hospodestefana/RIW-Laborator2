    IETF Hackathon Sponsorship

    US $40,000

    The IETF Hackathons embody the IETF mantra of running code, bringing together open standards and open source. IETF Hackathon 2017 sponsorship includes recognition and affiliation with all the IETF Hackathons in 2017, including those held in conjunction with each of the IETF Meetings in Chicago, Prague, and Singapore.

    IETF Hackathon sponsors receive:

    • Four (4) complimentary full conference registrations to each of the 2017 IETF meetings: $8,400 value
    • Company logo on the IETF Hackathon webpage
    • Acknowledgement at 2017 IETF meeting plenaries and proceedings
    • Company logo displayed on promotional materials, including event and Hackathon-specific signage, as well outreach materials to opensource and academic communities
    • Opportunity to designate a Co-chair for each IETF Hackathon in 2017
    • Social media acknowledgement recognizing company as the Hackathon sponsor
    • Recognition in the IETF Journal
