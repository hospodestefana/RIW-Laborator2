In this section Hide in this section Newcomers' tutorials Newcomers' training Key Info In addition to the Newcomers' training updated for each IETF meeting, Scott Bradner, who revised and presented this tutorial at the IETF for many years, produced a series of bite-sized IETF introductory videos specifically designed for online viewing for his Harvard University Extension School class. The Harvard Extension School graciously agreed to close caption the videos and let the IETF make them available for general viewing.
