In this section Hide in this section IETF Area overview tutorials Applications and Real-Time Area introduction Internet Area introduction Operations and Management Area introduction Routing Area introduction Security Area introduction Transport Area introduction Key Info TSV working groups cover core transport protocols, such as TCP, UDP, DCCP, and SCTP; congestion mitigation and notification; middlebox services, such as firewalls and IPv4/IPv6 network address translation; coordinating/transporting network storage, such as iSCSI, iFC, and NFS; and congestion avoidance by coordinating/optimizing traffic flows. The tutorial will give an overview of present work, briefly cover the history of the TSV area, and summarize current developments with respect to the future of the TSV area's work and organization.
