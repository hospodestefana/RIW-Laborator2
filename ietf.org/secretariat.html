    Key Info

    The IETF Administrative Director (IAD) has the day-to-day operational responsibility of providing fiscal and administrative support to the IETF through other activities, contractors and volunteers.

    The IETF Secretariat provides support for IETF activities, including events 

    The Internet Engineering Steering Group is directly responsible for the actions associated with entry into and movement along the Internet "standards track," including final approval of specifications as Internet Standards.

    Below is a list of active e-mail addresses/links for use in contacting the IETF Secretariat® team. Please use these addresses/URLs for the indicated purposes only. Please do not copy the email addresses in correspondence unless your message includes a request for IETF Secretariat® support. To contact the IETF Executive Director, please send a message to exec-director@ietf.org. 

    General/Administrative

    • ietf-info@ietf.org: For general information about the IETF
    • iesg-secretary@ietf.org: For administrative support (e.g. request publication of an I-D as an RFC, update milestones, ask questions about procedures, etc.)
    • I-D Submission Tool: To submit Internet-Drafts

    Meetings and Proceedings

    • meeting-planning@ietf.org: To coordinate IETF meetings with other meetings
    • Meeting Session Request Tool: To request meeting sessions at IETF meetings
    • agenda@ietf.org: To request BOF sessions at IETF meetings or inquire about your WG/RG session requests
    • ietf-registrar@ietf.org: For information on or assistance with registering for IETF meetings
    • Proceedings Tool: To submit agendas, minutes and presentation slides for IETF meeting sessions

    Sponsorship

    • sponsorship@ietf.org: To find out more about opportunities to sponsor the IETF and IETF events.

    Media

    • media@ietf.org: For media inquiries and general information about the IETF.

    Liaison and IPR Statements

    • Liaison Statement Tool or email statements@ietf.org

      mailto:statements@ietf.org

      : To submit liaison statements
    • IPR Disclosure Tool: To submit IPR disclosure statements

    Website Feedback

    • webmaster@ietf.org: To report outdated or incorrect content on the IETF website (including broken links) at www.ietf.org or to offer general suggestions on how site organization can be improved. 
    • See the website feedback page for additional information.

    Other Problem Reports

    • ietf-action@ietf.org: To report technical problems (e.g. problems related to servers, mailing lists, archives, web tools, etc.) or to offer suggestions
    • Contact Form: To report difficulty sending email to IETF mailing lists and/or the Secretariat
    • Copyright infringement: Follow the guidelines on the linked page to report 
    • Ombudsteam: To report potential harassment
